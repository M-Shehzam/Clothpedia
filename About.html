The Origin of Clothing

Clothing came From: Old English claðas "cloths; garments for the body," originally the plural of clað "cloth" (see cloth), which, in 19c., after the sense of "article of clothing" had mostly faded from it, acquired a new plural form, cloths, to distinguish it from this word.

Types of Clothing Qualities

Understanding different quality levels

Official articles from registered brands designed by professional designers. Amazing quality protected under copyright laws.

As you can get a general idea for the word ‘Original’ that it’s Official, also called ‘Original Articles’. You can get these Articles from a Brand, of course an Official Brand (Registered). These articles are designed by Professional-Designers for the Customers. The Quality of these Clothes are Amazing, Astonishing and of course not available anywhere else. Original articles are designed under the Copyright-Laws and Designer-Agreement, No one else can use these designs, if anyone does, he/she will be penalized under court. You can make your own Design and indeed it wil be Official and no one copy this Design without your Consent. You can buy these Articles from Brands like: Sui Dhaga, Adidas, Levis, Royal Ascort, etc.

Shop Online: Sui Dhaga | Levis

Professional copies of original articles. Almost same quality as originals but slightly cheaper. Made under contract with income sharing (5-10%).

King Copy or Replica (Copy), Normally used as ‘King-Copy’. It’s Kind of like Official means theres a But (Technicality), You can get these Types of Clothes from a Normal Brand, That Brand can or cannot be Officially Registered. Basically, these Replicas are a Professional copy of the original articles, Dealers of King-Copy Articles gets the Design from Original Brands or can be availed from any Registered Company. What happens is that the dealers sign a contract that can be at any terms of the company, for example it can be that little income from each suit is given to the company it can be at any percent, normally its between 5-10%. You can say that Quality of these clothes are almost as same as the Original-Articles but are a little cheaper, Sometimes there's a little difference but it cannot be Pointed out that easily (only by Professionalists) and sometimes there's not a Single Difference as it's the Exact same Copy. You can buy these articles from Brands like: Elo, Outfitters, One-Store, DFO, etc.

Shop Online: Outfitters | DFO

Not official but well-made copies with minor differences. Available online and in shops. Quality varies - A+ Master Replica being the best.

Master Replica Normally used as ‘Master-Copy’. It’s is Not Official Article from any side. These types of Designs are easily available from Online Shopping, Normal Shops, etc. Master Replicas are not that costly Basically, these Replicas are Mastered Copied from the Original Designs, but a with a little difference which is almost not Recognizable by a Normal Person/Customer, For example there could be a little Shade difference in Cloth, a small difference in Punching Design, a little difference in the angle of Embroidery or extra Embroidery at any point (it can be Soo little or fully obvious), a little Printing Difference in the angle of Printing or Some Extra Prining at some random point etc. Quality of these Clothes can Differ as from where you are purchasing it from some places it can almost be same as an Original in Stuff which we call 'A+ Master Replica', and from some places it can be Waste of Money. But it’s easy to identify that it’s a Master-Replica or not just by the Look of it; Model pic can be amazing, and you will probably have no doubt that the Stuff would be Amazing as well. Anyone can make them, but if you make the exact same copy as the Original Design then indeed you will be Penalized in Court. You can Purchase these Articles from Brands or Places like: Stylish Store, Bend the Trend, Daraz, Modern Designers, Small Brand Businesses, etc.

Shop Online: Ethno Threads | Modern Designers

Cheap copies with lower quality materials and embroidery. Affordable and usually worn at home. No copyright concerns as designs differ from originals.

Normal Replicas or Copies of the Original Dress are not so Good/Likeable Type of Clothing you can find in the Market. Basically, These Replicas are Copied from any Design, anyone can easily tell that it’s a Normal Copy or Replica just by looking at it, as not very good stuff of clothing is used and the embroidery, printed quality is not good at well. These type of Clothes are Cheap and don’t Cost that much, Easily Affordable by most of the People. These Designs are Normally Worn at Home. These Designs are also known as Cheap Copy. As These Designs are Different from Original Designs so you don’t have to Face any Charges. You can Purchase these Articles from Places like: Local Shops, Local Market, Fake (Non-Registered) Online Pages etc.

Cut-Pieces: Made from leftover pieces after dress completion. Mix and match from different designs.

In the Sense of Cut-Pieces, These Clothes are made up from the Pieces left behind after completion of a Dress (Extra Clothes). These Dresses can be a whole New Design after appending multiple cut-pieces together from a single or multiple dresses, For example you can take shirt/Kurti/Kurta from one Design, Pent/Trouser from another and Dupatta/Shawl from a whole another Design, after completion it can be Amazing or not even be able to Wear/See. You can Purchase these Dresses/Cut-Pieces from Places like: Factories, Local markets, Local Shops etc.

Used/Second-hand: Pre-owned clothing from around the world. Very affordable but quality varies. Available at local markets and thrift stores.

In the Sense of Used B-Type Clothing, these Designs are 2nd hand Clothing. As anyone can guess that these are used Cloths, these types of Clothes come from around the World, Quality of these Clothes are usually Torn, used, or sometimes Soo much used. These Clothes are Soo much cheap and Easy to buy for anyone. You can Purchase these Articles from Places like: Local markets, Thrift stores, Charity etc.
