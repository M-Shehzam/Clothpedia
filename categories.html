Categories

Categories of Clothing is Divided into Two Parts:

#1 Men

#2 Women


  • Men

Men Clothing can Include Shirts, Pants, 2-PC / 3-PC, Coats, Jackets, Hoods, Shawls, Sweaters, Kameez-Shalwar (Trouser), Dasha; etc.

Seasons:

  1. Summer
  2. Winter
  3. Spring
  4. Autumn

  • Summer:

Summer is a Hottest Season and as the word Hot explains itself, so any Men looking for dresses in this season can use Lightest Clothing variety in this season, some of the Types of Clothes Men can use in this Season are as Follows:

  1. Lawn
  2. Silk
  3. Denim

  • Winter:

Winter is a Cold season and as the word Cold explains itself, so any Men looking for dresses in this season can use Thick Clothes in this season, some of the Types of Clothes Men can use in this Season are as follows:

  1. Cotton
  2. Fleece
  3. Denim
  4. Wool

  • Spring:

Spring is a Mid-Season as its Generally Considered the Period between the Spring Equinox and the Summer Solstice, it’s between Winter and Summer during which Temperatures gradually increases, it’s basically the Best season to go out according to most of the people, So any Men looking for Dresses in this season can use Light / Thick Mix-Up Clothes (Not that Hot and not that Cold) in this season, some of the Types of Clothes Men can use in this Season are as Follows:

  1. Tropical Wool
  2. Eyelet
  3. Denim
  4. Wool
  5. Fleece
  6. Cotton

  • Autumn:

Autumn is a Mid-Season as it’s between Summer and Winter too, During This time of the Year, Temperatures gradually Decreases, so any Men looking for Dresses in this Season can start by light clothes and normally use Little Thick Clothes (Thicker the better) in this season, as the Temperature Decreases day by day, some of the Types of Clothing Men can use in this Season are as Follows:

  1. Denim
  2. Wool
  3. Cotton
  4. Fleece
